System Requirements
===================

This page outlines the top-level mission requirements for the QSET satellite. All specifications and implementations trace back to these.

Functional Requirements
-----------------------
.. req:: Mission Lifetime
   :id: REQ_SYS_101
   :status: approved

   The satellite shall operate successfully in orbit for at least 12 months.

.. req:: Communication Link
   :id: REQ_SYS_102
   :status: approved

   The satellite shall establish contact with the ground station at least once every 24 hours.

Physical Requirements
---------------------
.. req:: Mass Limit
   :id: REQ_SYS_103
   :status: approved

   The total satellite mass shall not exceed 4.0 kg.
